# Social Media Checklist for College Students

 Do's
-  Think before posting – consider how it reflects on you.
-  Use privacy settings to control who sees your content.
-  Cite sources when sharing academic or professional information.
-  Engage respectfully in discussions and debates.
-  Report inappropriate or harmful content.

 Don'ts
-  Share personal information like addresses or phone numbers publicly.
-  Post when angry or upset – avoid impulsive communication.
-  Spread rumors, misinformation, or unverified news.
-  Bully, harass, or demean others online.
-  Overshare details that could harm your reputation or future opportunities.

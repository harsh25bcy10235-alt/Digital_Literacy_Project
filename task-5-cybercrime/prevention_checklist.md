Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist
Use strong and unique passwords for all college portals, emails, and social media accounts. Utilize password managers to avoid password reuse.
Enable 2 Factor Authentication on Google accounts, college ERP accounts, banking apps, and social media accounts.
Avoid accessing banking accounts, UPI accounts, and email accounts via public Wi-Fi. Instead, use mobile hotspot or VPN.
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or QR code with anyone. Scammers may contact you in the name of verification.
Before sending money to anyone, call them and verify the request. Never scan the QR code to receive money.
Always check the permissions of the apps and remove any suspicious and unknown apps.
Be cautious of fake internship and part-time job offers that demand registration fees and ask you to click on unknown links.
Avoid sharing your personal pictures and ID proofs with unknown people. They may be misused for blackmail.
